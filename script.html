FAQ

How can I participate in Code Fest?

To join Code Fest, simply click on the 'Join Now' button and follow the registration process.

Are there any age restrictions for attendees?

Code Fest is open to all age groups, from coding prodigies to seasoned developers.

What should I bring to the event?

Bring your laptop, charger, and a curious mind ready to code!

Can I attend workshops without coding experience?

Absolutely! Our workshops cater to all skill levels, from beginners to experts.

How can I contact the organizers for more information?

For any inquiries, feel free to reach out to us at info@codefest.com.
